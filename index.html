    Review
    
        
    The engine of GTD. Daily for tactics, weekly for strategy, monthly for someday/maybe.
    
      
    
    
    

    
    
      
    Daily review (5 min)
    
      
    Run first thing in the morning or end of day.
    
      
    
      
    
    

    
    
      
    Weekly review (45-60 min)
    
      
    The keystone habit. Block a recurring slot — Friday afternoon or Monday morning works well.
    
      
    
      
    
    

    
    
      
    Monthly review (15 min)
    
      
    Higher altitude — areas of focus, someday/maybe, longer-term outcomes.
